# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this
project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation. We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open,
welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standards

Expected behavior: be respectful and considerate, welcome differing viewpoints,
give and gracefully accept constructive feedback, and focus on what is best for
the community.

Unacceptable behavior: harassment of any kind, personal or political attacks,
publishing others' private information without permission, and other conduct
which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)
as its full code of conduct; the link is the authoritative text.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may
remove, edit, or reject contributions that are not aligned with this Code of
Conduct.

Report unacceptable behavior **privately** by opening a
[GitHub Security Advisory](https://github.com/zakrian07/kumauptime/security/advisories/new)
or by contacting the maintainer via their GitHub profile (**@zakrian07**). All
reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and the reporter's
privacy and security will be respected.
